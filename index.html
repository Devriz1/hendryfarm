Meet Stella

Let us introduce you to the heart behind Hendry's Farm & Co — Stella. With a passion for nature and a vision for healthier living, Stella started this farm with one simple goal: to bring fresh, organic, and ethically produced food straight from the farm to your table. What began as a small dream has now blossomed into a trusted name in sustainable farming — all thanks to her dedication, love for animals, and commitment to quality.

Our Services

At Hendry's Farm & Co, we are committed to providing high-quality, sustainable products and experiences. Explore our core services below.

Fresh Egg Sales

We offer fresh, high-quality eggs directly from our farm. Our chickens are raised in a healthy, free-range environment, ensuring the best quality eggs for our customers.

Poultry Processing

Our poultry processing service ensures that our chickens are processed humanely and with the highest standards of hygiene. We offer a range of cuts and whole chickens, all processed on-site to maintain freshness and quality.

Farm Tours

Experience the heart of Golden Acres with our guided farm tours. Learn about our sustainable farming practices, meet our chickens, and enjoy the beautiful countryside. Tours are available by appointment only

About Us

About the Hendry's Farm & Co

Welcome to Hendry’s Farm & Co, your trusted source for farm-fresh chicken, organic eggs, and healthy hens. Founded by Stella, a dedicated farmer with a deep love for animals and sustainable agriculture, Hendry’s Farm began as a humble dream — to raise poultry the right way. With hands-on care and ethical practices, Stella built a farm where hens are treated like family and quality comes naturally. Our chickens are raised in open, clean environments, free from harmful chemicals or antibiotics. Every egg you receive is fresh, nutritious, and packed with the goodness that only nature can provide. At Hendry’s Farm & Co, we believe in honesty, hard work, and healthy living — one egg at a time.
